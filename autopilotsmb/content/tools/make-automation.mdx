Make (formerly Integromat) is the visual automation platform that lets you connect 1,500+ apps through a drag-and-drop canvas. More powerful than Zapier, with a lower price point.

## Why Make Stands Out

Make's visual canvas lets you see your entire automation at once — unlike Zapier's linear interface. This makes complex, multi-step workflows much easier to build and debug.

## Best Use Cases

- New lead → CRM + Slack notification + email sequence
- New Stripe payment → fulfillment + onboarding automation
- Blog post published → social media distribution
- Support ticket → classification + routing + notification

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1,000 operations/month, unlimited scenarios
- **Core ($9/month)**: 10,000 operations, scheduled automations
- **Pro ($16/month)**: 10,000 ops + full feature set

## Verdict

Best-in-class for complex automations. Start on the free plan and upgrade when you need more operations.
