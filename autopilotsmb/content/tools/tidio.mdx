## Why Lyro AI is Different

Unlike rule-based chatbots that require you to manually program every Q&A, Lyro reads your existing support documentation and website, then answers questions in natural language. Setup takes minutes.

## Best Use Cases

- E-commerce stores with repetitive order/shipping questions
- SaaS with FAQ-heavy onboarding
- Service businesses managing after-hours inquiries
- Any business handling 20+ support tickets per week
