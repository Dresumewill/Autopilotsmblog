Canva is the design tool that turned everyone into a designer. With Pro, its AI features (Magic Design, Magic Write, text-to-image) let you create professional visual content at a pace that was previously impossible.

## AI Features in Canva Pro

- **Magic Design**: Describe what you want, get a ready-to-edit design
- **Magic Write**: AI copywriting built into your designs
- **Magic Eraser/Edit**: Remove backgrounds, objects, and resize images
- **Brand Kit**: Maintain consistent colors, fonts, logos across everything

## Best Use Cases

- Social media graphics for all platforms (auto-resizes)
- Presentation decks and pitch materials
- Newsletter headers and email graphics
- Thumbnails, banners, and ad creatives

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic templates, limited storage
- **Pro ($15/month)**: Full AI suite, 100M+ premium assets, brand kit, background remover

## Verdict

The free plan is surprisingly capable. Upgrade to Pro when you need the brand kit, background remover, and premium template library.
