Jasper is purpose-built for marketing teams that need to produce consistent, on-brand content at scale. Unlike general AI tools, Jasper's strength is learning and maintaining your specific brand voice.

## Best Use Cases

- Email sequences and newsletter campaigns
- Google and Meta ad copy at scale
- Landing page copy and A/B test variations
- Product descriptions for e-commerce

## Pricing

- **Creator ($49/month)**: 1 user, brand voice, 1 seat
- **Pro ($69/month)**: 5 seats, 10 brand voices, campaigns feature

## Verdict

Worth it for teams producing 50+ pieces of marketing content per month. Solo operators should start with Claude at $20/month.
