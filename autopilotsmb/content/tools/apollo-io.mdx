Apollo is the sales intelligence platform that gives small businesses the same prospecting capabilities that used to require enterprise-level sales tools. With 275M+ verified contacts and built-in email sequencing, it's a complete outbound sales stack.

## Key Features

- **Contact Database**: 275M+ contacts with verified emails and phone numbers
- **Filters**: Search by title, industry, company size, location, revenue, and 65+ more
- **Email Sequences**: Automated multi-step outreach with A/B testing
- **AI Email Writing**: Generate personalized first lines at scale
- **CRM Integration**: Sync with HubSpot, Salesforce, and others

## Best Use Cases

- B2B solopreneurs doing cold outreach for client acquisition
- Agencies prospecting for new clients
- SaaS founders doing founder-led sales
- Consultants targeting specific company types

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 email credits/month, basic filters
- **Basic ($49/month)**: 1,000 credits, full filters, sequences
- **Professional ($79/month)**: Unlimited email, phone credits, advanced AI

## Verdict

Essential for any B2B business doing outbound. The free plan is too limited, but the Basic plan is excellent value for small teams.
