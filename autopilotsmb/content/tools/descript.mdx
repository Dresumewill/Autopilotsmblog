Descript is the most innovative video editor for non-editors. Instead of a timeline, you edit a transcript — delete a word and the video cut follows. It changes everything about how solopreneurs approach video content.

## Key AI Features

- **Transcription**: Accurate, word-level transcription
- **Filler word removal**: Remove ums and uhs in one click
- **Overdub**: Fix mistakes by typing new words in your own voice
- **Studio Sound**: Remove background noise and improve audio quality
- **Social clips**: Auto-generate short clips from long recordings

## Best Use Cases

- Online course creators editing lecture videos
- Podcasters doing episode editing and show notes
- Business owners repurposing long-form content into clips
- Marketers creating video testimonials and case studies
