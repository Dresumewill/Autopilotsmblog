If you're already using Notion, adding AI is a no-brainer. If you're not using Notion yet, it's one of the best business operating systems for solopreneurs and small teams.

## Notion AI Capabilities

- **Summarize**: Turn 30-minute meeting notes into a 5-bullet action list
- **Draft**: Generate SOP drafts, project briefs, or proposals from bullet points
- **Fill AI blocks**: Auto-generate content for any block in your workspace
- **Q&A**: Ask questions about your Notion content and get instant answers

## Best Use Cases

- Meeting notes → action items → team updates
- Project brief generation from rough ideas
- SOP documentation from process descriptions
- Weekly and monthly business reviews

## Pricing

- **Free plan**: Collaborative workspace, no AI
- **AI add-on ($10/member/month)**: Added to any paid plan
- **Plus + AI ($18/month)**: Full workspace + AI

## Verdict

Essential if Notion is your operating system. The AI features pay for themselves on day one for teams doing a lot of documentation.
